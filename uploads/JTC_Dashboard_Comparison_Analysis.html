Issues with Word Document Format

  • Members unaware of total scope until document review
  • No immediate understanding of workload
  • Difficult to cross-reference tender numbers
  • Time-consuming sequential reading required
  • No quick navigation between sections
  • Limited visual differentiation between items
  • Risk of missing important information
  • Inefficient for informed decision-making
  • Static format cannot be updated during meeting
  • No status indicators or visual cues

Benefits of Dashboard Solution

  • Instant overview of all activities
  • Clear visual metrics at first glance
  • One-click navigation to any section
  • Interactive tender number linking
  • Professional, modern presentation
  • Color-coded department identification
  • Status indicators for quick understanding
  • Efficient information flow
  • Enhanced decision-making capability
  • Reduced meeting time
